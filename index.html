About MVisioner

An AI Robotics Company Born in Hong Kong

Founded in 2015 with operations in Hong Kong and Shenzhen, MVisioner develops AI-driven robotic systems powered by its proprietary PALV (Perception-Action-Language-Visualization) platform. The same foundational AI stack — computer vision, embodied intelligence, natural language, and immersive visualization — delivers sub-millimeter precision in the operating room and emotionally expressive motion in the living room. ISO 13485-certified, with CDRMS (Contract Design, Research, Manufacturing & Servicing) capabilities from R&D through commercialization.

PALV Platform

Proprietary Perception-Action-Language-Visualization stack that unifies vision (SAM, YOLO), embodied AI (V-JEPA, VLA), language (LLM, TTS, ASR), and immersive visualization (VR/AR, naked-eye 3D) into one deployable framework.

Two Domains, One Stack

Medical robotics demanding sub-millimeter precision and consumer robotics requiring natural, flexible interaction — powered by the same core AI.

CDRMS & Manufacturing

Product Platforms

Two Domains. One Intelligence.

MVisioner's PALV platform powers robots at both extremes — the unforgiving precision of surgery and the natural flexibility of human companionship.

Medical Robotics — High Precision

Meridian Surgical Robotics

PALV's Perception layer provides real-time anatomical landmark recognition and 3D semantic segmentation for surgical navigation. The Action layer delivers 7-axis robotic control with force feedback and sub-millimeter accuracy. The Visualization layer powers one of the few VR/AR surgical planning platforms for orthopedics and naked-eye 3D intraoperative display. The Language layer enables voice-driven surgical documentation and AI planning assistance.

MV-01
Spinal Surgical Robotic Guidance System

Robot-assisted pedicle screw insertion and MIMS surgery. Supports spine, hip, knee, and trauma indications. Compatible with O-ARM, 3D C-Arm, and 2D C-Arm.

MV-02
EndoSpineAssist Robotic Navigation System

Specialized robotic navigation for UBE endoscopic spine procedures.

MV-03
Neurology Robotic System

Precision robotic guidance for neurological surgical procedures.

SurgiVision VIS-01
3D Display & AI Fusion System

Glass-free naked-eye 3D visualization with multi-modal medical image and video fusion for surgical theatres. Reduces surgeon fatigue and improves spatial coordination.

VR Planning
XR Surgical Planning Platform

Immersive VR/AR preoperative planning for orthopedic and spinal procedures — one of the few VR planning platforms available for orthopedics. Enables surgeons to rehearse procedures in 3D with patient-specific anatomy before entering the OR.

Consumer Robotics — High Flexibility

Lumie — Emotionally Intelligent AI Companion

PALV's Perception layer enables real-time visual understanding — face recognition, emotion detection, and scene context. The Action layer drives 5-DoF expressive motion with Pixar-like fluidity via the Emotion-Motion Engine. The Language layer delivers natural conversation with an on-device 0.7B LLM and cloud escalation for complex queries.

Perception — Vision AI
Real-Time Visual Understanding

SAM-class zero-shot segmentation and YOLO-family real-time object detection for emotion recognition and scene understanding. Enables Lumie to recognize users, track objects, and interpret visual context at the edge.

Action — Embodied Intelligence
Motion Planning & Control

V-JEPA/VLA-inspired visuomotor action models drive the 5-DoF robotic arm with Pixar-like fluidity. The Emotion-Motion Engine translates intent into expressive, lifelike gestures — nods, shrugs, dances, and adaptive postures.

LLM + TTS + ASR Fusion
